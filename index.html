    Popular books
    LORE

    By Alexandra Bracken. From the #1 New York Times best-selling author of The Darkest Minds comes a sweepingly ambitious, high-octane tale of power, destiny, love and redemption.

    Red Queen

    By Victoria Aveyard. Red Queen is a young adult dystopian fantasy romance novel written by American writer Victoria Aveyard.Published in February 2015, it was her first novel and first series

    Cruel Prince

    By Holly Black. The Cruel Prince (2018) follows Jude Duarte, a mortal girl living in Elfhame, a faerie world. Swept against her will to Elfhame, Jude and her twin sister, Taryn must adapt to living alongside powerful creatures with a deep disdain for humans and a penchant for violent delights.


    Queen of Air and Darkness

    By Cassandra Clare . Queen of Air and Darkness is the third and final book in The Dark Artifices trilogy by Cassandra Clare, which is the fourth chronological series in The Shadowhunter Chronicles.

    Shadow Me

    By Tahereh Mafi. Shadow Me is the third novella in the Shatter Me series by Tahereh Mafi. It was released on March 5, 2019. It bridges the gap between Restore Me and Defy Me.

    Amid Cloud and Bones

    By Ella Fields. A princess determined to survive an arranged marriage to a heartless faerie prince is saved by a twist of fate that could destroy their world in this all-new standalone fantasy romance.
